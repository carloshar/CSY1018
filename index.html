About me

This post was written by Carl.H 12/Nov/2017

My name is Carl Harrison and I was born in Milton Keynes and live in Dunstable but am currently studying computing at the University of Northampton. I love to learn understanding something new every day is what I want in my future job, the job I want to attain is as a software engineer. I’m 18 and this changes every year on the 15th of May.

My hobbies include Fishing, I go to the canal mainly catch perch and bream and sometimes a very annoying crayfish. Another hobby is pub games (pool & darts) I grew up playing these games with my dad every weekend when we would pop down to the local club so love to play a few games whenever I get the chance. My last big hobby would be Rugby I played for years but stopped during my exams, I was hoping to pick it back up during university but am finding myself too busy to spare anytime as I also have a job while studying.

My plans in later years would be to complete my degree with the best grade I can attain to get a job in software engineering. I would like to live in America or Canada depending on the president at my time of moving and build my own house with my own designs.
